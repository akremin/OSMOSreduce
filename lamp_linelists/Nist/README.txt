Spectral Lamp ThAr list is from https://physics.nist.gov/cgi-bin/TH/site.cgi

Spectral Lines are from https://physics.nist.gov/cgi-bin/ASD/lines1.pl

ThAr comes from the 20mA NIST_ThAr_20mA_air_linelist.csv where lines near a calibrated 
M2FS peak were kept, in addition to lines with reported fluxes greater than the 25 percentile
of the other peaks selected.
